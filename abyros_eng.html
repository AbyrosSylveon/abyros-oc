Demonic Dossier

Conscious Demon Essence

Abyros is the ultimate anomaly: a being who died and returned not as a ghost, but as a living demon. His existence is a direct response to the massive trauma caused by Lucario's betrayal. The fall into the Hell of Violence didn't destroy him; it completely reforged him.

His presence alters the behavior of living beings within a 30-meter radius, generating inexplicable anxiety and the sensation of being watched. His SS+ classification reflects his absolute dangerousness and complete dissociation from any form of compassion or conventional morality.

Fragmented Psychology: Sarcasm as Weapon

Despite his brutal nature, Abyros maintains a cruel irony. He mocks his victims, reminds them of their mistakes, and enjoys seeing the fear in their eyes. This behavior is not gratuitous sadism, but a way of processing his own trauma: turning others into victims as he once was.

Behavioral Patterns and Detected Mannerisms

Exhibits extreme dependence on fresh blood to maintain mental and physical stability. Without regular executions, his mind fragments and loses total control. His sadistic and mocking behavior usually precedes an execution, using fear as a primary psychological weapon.

Deeply hates any form of betrayal or lies, reacting with extreme hostility to these stimuli. The "90-degree head tilt" is a weakness analysis protocol, not a casual gesture. Obsessively cleans his sword even during combat.

Chronicle of the Fall and Resurrection

Before the abyss, Abyros was a Shiny Sylveon destined to inherit an ancestral throne. His deep empathy and shy nature made him the perfect candidate to lead with compassion. But his best friend, Lucario, poisoned by envy and ambition, couldn't bear to live in his shadow.

The betrayal was brutal: a blow from behind that shattered his left eye, followed by a push into the abyss that connects directly to the Hell of Violence. Lucario believed Abyros would die. He was terribly wrong.

For countless decades, hell tore apart his body, fragmented his mind, and transformed his soul. He learned that blood is power, that killing maintains sanity, and that violence is not an act but a language. His white fur stained gray and then crimson red. His empathy died and the demon was born.

When he finally emerged, he was no longer the shy heir. He was the Angel of Violence, a living-corrupted entity with a single purpose: to find Lucario and show him exactly what monster his betrayal had created. Every execution is practice. Every death is one step closer to ultimate vengeance.

BLOOD HARVEST Protocol: Total Absorption

The Bloodreaper is not just a weapon; it's a parasitic entity that chose Abyros as its bearer. Every contact with blood activates its total drain capacity, absorbing 100% of the target's vital energy in a process painfully slow for the victim but instantaneous for external observers.

The sword feeds and feeds Abyros simultaneously, creating a cycle of mutual dependence. Without it, Abyros loses 60% of his power. Without Abyros, the sword returns to its inert state awaiting the next worthy executioner.

CRIMSON JUDGMENT Protocol: Blood Sentence

In ritual execution mode, the Bloodreaper channels all previously absorbed blood to create a single devastating blow that not only kills the body, but fragments the target's soul. Victims experience all the pain Abyros suffered in hell condensed into a single eternal second.

This protocol only activates when Abyros has determined the victim deserves absolute punishment. It is reserved exclusively for traitors and those whose existence he considers "noise in the system of justice".
